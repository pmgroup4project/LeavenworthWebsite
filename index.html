About Leavenworth

Leavenworth, WA, is a charming Bavarian-style village nestled in the Cascade Mountains. Known for its stunning scenery, unique shops, and family-friendly atmosphere, it's a place you'll want to visit again and again.

Icicle Village Resort

The Icicle Village Resort offers a complete vacation experience with its lounge, game room, swimming pool, hot tubs, and a fun mini putt course. Enjoy breathtaking mountain views and make unforgettable family memories.

Best Burger in Town

Right across from the resort, there's a small burger joint that serves some of the best burgers you'll ever taste. It's the perfect spot to grab a bite after a day of exploring.

Explore Downtown Leavenworth

Just a short walk from the Icicle Village Resort, downtown Leavenworth is a must-visit with its charming shops, delicious restaurants, and beautiful Bavarian architecture.
